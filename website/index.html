Documentation

Open Google Cloud Console, create a new project, and enable the Google Drive API. Create OAuth credentials and select Desktop app (this is required for dynamic loopback ports on Android).

While the app is in "Testing" mode on Google Cloud, ensure your personal Google email is added to the "Test users" list under the OAuth consent screen.

Open CassielDrive, navigate to Settings, and paste your generated Client ID and Client Secret. After saving, you can click "Add Account" to authenticate.
